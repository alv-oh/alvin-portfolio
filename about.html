About Me

The story behind the code.

Who I Am

I'm Alvin, a second-year Bachelor of Informatics and Computer Science (BICS) student at Strathmore University in Nairobi. I spend most of my time building full-stack applications, working through coursework in distributed systems and data structures, and figuring out how software can solve problems that actually matter to people around me.

My biggest project so far is AirtimeX, a fintech app that lets users convert airtime into M-Pesa mobile money. I built the whole thing — React Native frontend, Node.js backend, PostgreSQL database — and shipped it as a working Android app.

Outside of coding, I'm interested in AI/ML and how it intersects with African contexts, and I write about culture and identity when I'm not debugging something at 1am.
